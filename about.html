About Us

Evanston is a trusted supplier of high-quality medical equipment, committed to delivering reliable and efficient healthcare solutions. We work with reputable manufacturers to ensure every product meets strict quality standards. Our goal is to make advanced medical technology accessible and affordable. We focus on building long-term relationships through excellent service and customer satisfaction.

Company Overview

Evanston Medical Limited was founded in 2012 with a vision to provide top-tier medical and aesthetic equipment to healthcare professionals worldwide. Headquartered in London, UK, we specialize in manufacturing, sales, technical services, training, and OEM/ODM solutions.

With state-of-the-art production facilities and a dedicated R&D team, we develop advanced medical devices including Cryolipolysis, Laser Systems, HIFU, Shockwave Therapy, Pressotherapy, and multi-functional aesthetic platforms. Our products are trusted by hospitals, clinics, and beauty centers across Europe, Americas, Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

We operate under strict ISO standards and hold CE, FDA, and other international certifications. Our commitment to innovation, quality control, and customer satisfaction has made Evanston Medical a leading name in the medical aesthetics industry.
